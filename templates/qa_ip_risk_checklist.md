# IP / Policy Risk Checklist (Minimum Standard)

Reject or rewrite if any apply:
- Direct use of a brand, celebrity, trademark, or protected character
- “Looks like” / “inspired by” close imitation of a specific known property
- Quotes strongly associated with a living author/public figure
- Sports team marks, school marks, corporate logos
- Medical/financial promises that imply outcomes

Acceptable:
- Generic themes
- Original phrasing
- Broad cultural references without identifiable ownership
- Public domain where verified and clean
